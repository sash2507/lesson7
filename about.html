Our Mission

We have a dual mission to:
be Wonder Woman's champion and support her ideals of justice, peace, equality, and to support her amazing technology!

Who is Wonder Woman?

  • Wonder Woman exists! Her secret identity is Diana Prince
  • Wonder Woman is from Paradise Island (that's in Ancient Greece!)
  • Wonder Woman has run for president twice (but hasn't won). Maybe she should try for 2016
  • Her first TV appearance was in 1974
  • Her awesome powers come from Greek gods

Our Vision

Our vision is to help girls save the world! That means teaching them strength, independence, compassion & non-discrimination - all through Wonder Woman's heroic acts.
